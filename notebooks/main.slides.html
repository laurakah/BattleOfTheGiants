Summary¶

Is India going to be a future superpower in Data Science?¶

Based on the analysis of selected features we showed, that India has the potential to be an upcoming Data Science superpower and can have a high impact on the future of Data Science globally.

  1. The ratio of Indian participants in the survey grew compared to the ratio of US participants by 20% from 2017 to 2019. Assuming these Indian participants are interested, apply or support digital technologies they increase their contrubution to digital sciences.
  2. Most of the Indian participants are younger (<25 years) and have less experience in Data Science and Machine Lerning than US participants with the same age, but they provide twice as many young developers.
  3. Indian participants are morelikley to have a lower education degree than US participants. This could be derived by the assumption, that for learning Data Science nowdays you just need a digital device and no educational infrastructure.

How is the state of the art (status quo) in US and India regarding programming languages, technologies and Machine Learning algorithms?¶

  1. Within both India and the US Python is the programming language most participants use. In the US higher percentages of programmers use SQL, R and Bash than in India.
